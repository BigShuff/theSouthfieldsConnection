About The Southfields Connection

Hi my name is Steve my wife and I have been living in Southfields since August 2000. I am a Website Engineer (semi retired). I developed this website in the hope that you the people of the community may find it a benefit in the following ways.

  • By holding safe public walks and coffee mornings etc, ensuring that everyone in the community has events where they can find and talk to others.
  • Supplying a local for sale and wanted section. Enabling us to give new life to items that would otherwise end up unwanted or thrown away.
  • Handy community information at your fingertips.
  • A regular crossword along with readers recipies, and interesting stories for your free time.
  • Local business advertising helping us to find someone local to help us with those jobs we cant do ourselves.

As well as other helpful regular sections, all for free or in the case of advertisements at extremely affordable prices. The top and bottom of it is we are the Southfields Connection and we endevour to connect both people and communities.
